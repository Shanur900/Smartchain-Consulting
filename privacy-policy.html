Smartchain Privacy and Cookie Policy

Privacy Policy

Smart chain’s privacy policy is to respect your privacy regarding all the details and information that we collect while using our website. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting personally identifiable information that you provide while filling in the details and providing confidential information.

Our privacy policy explains what information we collect on our website, how we can use your details, and under what situations we will disclose your details to third parties. Our policy only adheres to the information we collect through the website.

Along with terms and conditions, our privacy policy set forth the general rules and policies that show how you can use our website. Depending on your activities when visiting our Website, you may be required to agree to additional terms and conditions.

Website Visitors

Smart Chain collects non-personally identifying information that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, referring site, preference of the language, details like date and time of each visitor.

Our purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand the behavior of our visitors and how they use our website. We may release non-personally identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.

Also, we collect potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses for logged-in users and for users leaving comments on the blog section of our website. We might disclose user and commenter IP addresses that only under special circumstances.

If a visitor wants to use the website, they must accept the privacy policy. If you do not agree to any portion of the policy as a whole, you can then move back from the website.

Gathering of Personally-Identifying Information

It has been observed that there are some visitors who want to interact with Smart Chain in a certain way that requires us to personally collect confidential details. The amount and type of information that we collect largely depend on the nature of the interaction.

For example, we may ask visitors to provide a user name and email address who signs up for a blog.

Security

Since our inception, security has been a prime concern to us and we harness state-of-the-art technology to keep your information secret. But it couldn’t be wrong to say that no method of exchange of information on the internet is 100% secure.

Although we consistently endeavor to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee you that it will be 100% safe and secured.

Advertisements

Advertisements on our websites will be shown to the users who may have accepted cookies. These cookies allow the ad server to recognize your computer each time they send you an online advertisement to accumulate information about you or others who use your laptop or desktop.

If you are interested in buying a product or a service and you have searched for them before on the internet, these ad servers will show you the advertisements. This Privacy Policy, however, does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.

Links to External Sites

Our Service may contain links that are directed to the external sites. You will be directed to that third party’s site if you accidentally click on that link. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy and terms and conditions every time you visit the website. And the reason behind this is that we have no control over the content, privacy policies, and practices of any third parties, services, or products.

So when you visit our website, you will need to agree to the respective terms and conditions.

Aggregated Statistics

Smart Chain may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors like what they are searching on the website, what they are clicking at, and how much time spend on our website. But we never disclose your personally-identifying information to any third-party.

Cookies

Smart Chain uses “Cookies”, similar technologies and services to offer you a seamless online experience. By your behavior, we display personalized content, appropriate advertising and store your preferences on your computer.

A cookie is nothing but a series of information that a website store on a visitor’s computer and the browser of the visitor provide the information to the website whenever they visit. We efficiently use cookies to identify and track visitors, and how they use our website

Before using the website if you do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers, you must refuse cookies. But if you do so, you may not view or operate the website the way you want.

When you browse our website without making any changes to the cookie settings, you already agree to the use of cookies.

Privacy Policy Changes

As per the latest government norms, we constantly change our privacy policy from time to time. We thus encourage visitors to frequently check this page to track any changes made under the section of the privacy policy.

Even after any change in this Privacy Policy if you continue using the website, then you have automatically accepted such changes.
